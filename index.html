Registration & Change Policy Timeline

Available actions by deadline stage — check this first before processing any request

▾
⛔ Hard Rules — Applies at All Stages, No Exceptions
Rule 1 · Universal Restrictions
  • 🚫Individual division: no athlete replacement under any circumstance.
  • 🚫No division / category changes for Individual, Doubles, or Relay.
  • 🚫No deferrals after the deferral deadline — no exceptions regardless of reason.
  • 🚫No refund processing after race day — all refund requests must be submitted before or on race day.
Rule 2 · Non-Medical Changes — One Change Per Order, Ever
  • 📌Each order is allowed one non-medical change only. This covers: deferral, captain change, or division change due to teammate injury.
  • 🚫Once a change has been made, no further changes will be accepted at any stage — through to race day.
  • 🚫The following are not accepted as grounds for any change or replacement: traffic delays, flight disruptions, work commitments, or finding a new teammate on-site.
Rule 3 · Medical Cases — Special Handling Regardless of Prior Changes
  • 🏥Medical cases are handled separately regardless of whether a prior change has been made. A valid medical certificate (issued to the athlete personally, 本人) must be provided before race day.
  • 👤Addie reviews the certificate. If verified → offer 50% medical refund.
  • 👥If the athlete has found a replacement teammate: direct them to the onsite Help Desk. CS will assess based on current busyness and start time availability. If accommodation is not possible → offer 50% medical refund.

Post-Race

Wrap-Up, Shipping & Review

▾
① Return to HQ

Ship all Information Desk items back to Headquarters. Contact athletes and arrange return of Lost & Found items.

② Ship to Athletes

Ship finisher patches (hook & loop and fridge magnet) and Lost & Found items to athletes.

③ Flag Delivery
  • If official rankings change after post-race review, adjustments are made based on the final official results.
  • Athletes whose final ranking places them in the top 3 will receive the correct podium flag. If the final ranking is 1st place, a replacement thermal cup will also be sent.
  • For incorrectly issued flags, we will contact athletes to request return — but this is not mandatory. Decisions will consider whether the athlete has departed, international shipping costs, and their willingness to cooperate.
  • If an athlete holds a wrong flag and their own final ranking also entitles them to a new flag, the correct flag will be sent once the original is returned. If they decline to return it, no replacement will be issued.
  • Thermal cups already issued will not be recalled.
④ Review All Issues

Summarize and review all on-site issues. Conduct a post-event debrief and improvement review.

⑤ Sponsor Feedback

If feedback relates to on-site sponsor materials:

  • Verify with Elisa & Melody
  • Handle individual cases separately
  • If multiple similar issues → escalate to Melody & sponsor

Government Authority Complaint Handling

Time-independent workflow — initiated upon receipt of official inquiry

Always Active
▾
🏛️ Triggered by: Official government / regulatory contact ⏱ Priority: High — respond promptly 📁 Language: English
1

Receive & Investigate the Complaint

Upon receiving official contact from a government authority, review the work order / inquiry in full. Then locate and examine all relevant records pertaining to the named athlete(s) or spectator(s):

💬Chat & communication logs with the individual
🎫Order information and registration records
📋On-site incident or issue logs
🗂️Any prior CS interaction history

Goal: Build a complete factual picture of the case before drafting any response.

2

Prepare a Formal Complaint Response

Draft the response using the official Complaint Explanation Template. The submission must include all supporting evidence compiled in Step 1:

📸Evidence screenshots (timestamped where possible)
💬Relevant chat / communication records
🎫Order confirmation & transaction information
📄Any additional documentation supporting the account
📌

Adhere strictly to the template structure. Do not include speculative statements or opinions — all claims must be substantiated by the attached evidence.

3

Issue Conclusion Based on Company Policy

Review the evidence against internal regulations and policies. Formulate an official conclusion that:

  • States clearly whether the complaint is upheld, partially upheld, or not upheld
  • References the specific company rule(s) or policy clause(s) applied
  • Explains the outcome in plain, professional language suitable for official correspondence
  • Is reviewed and approved by the appropriate team lead before submission

Before Race – Part 1

Allowed Actions Before the Change Deadline

▾
Request Type A

📅 1. Postponement Request

1

Confirm Athlete Details

Confirm the following with the athlete and record in the "Change Log":

  • Order number
  • Athlete information
  • Which race to postpone to
2

Process Refund in Ticketing System

In the ticketing system, process a refund for the original order:

  • Refund amount: 0 USD
  • Refund reason: Postponement
3

Issue Discount Code

Issue the corresponding discount code (by race category) from the Change Log to the athlete — do this before tickets go on sale for the new race.

Request Type B

💰 2. Refund Request

Athletes must request a refund by themselves via the official mini program. No CS intervention required.

Government Authority Complaint Handling

Time-independent workflow — initiated upon receipt of official inquiry

Always Active
▾
🏛️ Triggered by: Official government / regulatory contact ⏱ Priority: High — respond promptly 📁 Language: English
1

Receive & Investigate the Complaint

Upon receiving official contact from a government authority, review the work order / inquiry in full. Then locate and examine all relevant records pertaining to the named athlete(s) or spectator(s):

💬Chat & communication logs with the individual
🎫Order information and registration records
📋On-site incident or issue logs
🗂️Any prior CS interaction history

Goal: Build a complete factual picture of the case before drafting any response.

2

Prepare a Formal Complaint Response

Draft the response using the official Complaint Explanation Template. The submission must include all supporting evidence compiled in Step 1:

📸Evidence screenshots (timestamped where possible)
💬Relevant chat / communication records
🎫Order confirmation & transaction information
📄Any additional documentation supporting the account
📌

Adhere strictly to the template structure. Do not include speculative statements or opinions — all claims must be substantiated by the attached evidence.

3

Issue Conclusion Based on Company Policy

Review the evidence against internal regulations and policies. Formulate an official conclusion that:

  • States clearly whether the complaint is upheld, partially upheld, or not upheld
  • References the specific company rule(s) or policy clause(s) applied
  • Explains the outcome in plain, professional language suitable for official correspondence
  • Is reviewed and approved by the appropriate team lead before submission

✔ If Complaint Not Upheld

Provide a clear explanation of the policy and evidence showing the company acted appropriately. Maintain a respectful, professional tone throughout.

⚠ If Complaint Upheld / Partial

Acknowledge the issue, state the remedial action taken or proposed, and outline any process improvements where relevant. Escalate to management as appropriate.
